How to Build a Business Website in 10 Minutes with AI

Beegen Team April 22, 2026 6 min read

Not long ago, getting a business website meant one of three things: hiring a developer (expensive), learning WordPress (time-consuming), or using a drag-and-drop builder (frustrating). The average small business spent anywhere from $2,000 to $10,000 and several weeks just to get a basic five-page site live.

That math has completely changed. With AI website builders, you can go from "I need a website" to a live, professional site in the time it takes to drink a cup of coffee. Here's exactly how to do it.

What You'll Need

Almost nothing. Seriously. Before you start, have these three things ready:

  • A clear description of your business — what you do, who you serve, and what makes you different
  • Your logo (optional, but helpful) — a PNG or SVG file
  • Your brand colors (optional) — even a rough idea like "navy blue and gold" works fine

That's it. No hosting account. No domain purchase required to start. No FTP credentials or WordPress plugins.

Step-by-Step: From Zero to Live in 10 Minutes

1

Sign up and get your free credits

Go to app.beegen.co and create a free account with your email. You'll get 3 credits immediately — no credit card required. Each credit generates one complete website, so you have three attempts to get something you love.

2

Write your first prompt

This is the most important step, and it's simpler than you think. Describe your business like you'd explain it to a friend. The more specific you are, the better the result. We'll cover exactly what to include in a moment.

3

Review and refine

The AI generates a complete website in about 30 seconds. If something's off — wrong tone, missing a section, headline doesn't quite land — just send another message describing what to change. You're having a conversation, not filling out a form.

4

Upload your logo and set brand colors

In the brand settings panel, upload your logo and optionally specify your brand colors. The AI will restyle the entire site to match your visual identity in one more generation.

5

Publish

Hit publish and your site goes live instantly on a yoursite.beegen.co subdomain. If you're on Starter or higher, you can connect your own custom domain from the dashboard.

How to Write a Prompt That Gets Great Results

The single biggest factor in your result quality is how well you describe what you want. Here's a simple formula:

[Business type] + [Who you serve] + [What makes you different] + [Key pages or sections] + [Tone/vibe]

Here's an example of a weak prompt versus a strong one:

Weak: "A website for my photography business."

Strong: "A website for a wedding and portrait photographer based in Austin, TX. I've been shooting for 8 years and specialize in candid, documentary-style photography — not overly posed. My clients are couples aged 25–40 who value authenticity over perfection. I need a homepage with a hero image gallery, an About section, a Services page with pricing starting at $2,400, and a Contact form. Tone should feel warm, personal, and a little artsy — not corporate."

The strong version gives the AI enough context to make real creative decisions. The result will look like it was made specifically for your business, not a generic template.

What the AI Can and Can't Do

Being honest here: AI website builders are excellent at structure, copy, and visual design. They're less good at replacing your specific portfolio photos or real customer testimonials. Here's how to think about it:

  • AI handles well: layout, navigation structure, placeholder copy, color schemes, typography, CTA placement, mobile responsiveness
  • You'll want to customize: real images, your specific testimonials and case studies, pricing details, team bios

Think of the AI as handling the 80% that most people spend 80% of their time on. You add the final 20% of personalization that makes it truly yours.

What Does It Cost?

You can build and preview for free with 3 credits — no credit card needed. To publish a live site, the Starter plan is $19/month and includes 30 credits. That's about 30 websites a month, or one website with 29 rounds of refinements.

For comparison, the cheapest professional web designer typically charges $500–$1,500 for a basic site, plus ongoing maintenance fees. For a freelancer or small business owner, the math is obvious.

The Honest Bottom Line

AI website builders aren't magic. They won't replace a fully custom-built web application with complex functionality. But for the overwhelming majority of small businesses, freelancers, and creators who just need a professional online presence — a homepage, an about page, a contact form, maybe a services or portfolio page — they've completely changed what's possible.

Ten minutes. No code. No designer. No excuses.
