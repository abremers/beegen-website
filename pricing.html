Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans and billing.

Your free trial gives you full access to all features on the Business plan for 14 days — no restrictions, no watermarks, no paywalls. You can build your site, create funnels, set up your membership area, and connect your custom domain. Everything you'd get as a paying customer is available from day one.
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your account dashboard — no phone call required, no hoops to jump through. If you cancel, you'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
No credit card is required to start your free trial. Sign up with just your email address and start building immediately. You'll only be asked for payment details when you decide to upgrade to a paid plan.
When your 14-day trial ends, your account will be paused — not deleted. Your data, pages, and funnels are all saved. You'll see a prompt to choose a plan to reactivate. We'll send you reminders a few days before so you're never caught off guard.
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account settings. When you upgrade, the change is effective immediately and you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date.
No setup fees, ever. The price you see on the pricing page is exactly what you pay — nothing hidden, nothing added at checkout. We believe in complete pricing transparency.
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied for any reason within 14 days of your first payment, contact our support team and we'll process a full refund — no questions asked.
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers.
