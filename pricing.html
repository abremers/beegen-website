How credits work

What's a credit?

One message = one credit

Each message you send to the AI uses 1 credit. The AI then generates or updates your entire website based on your instruction.

Complex requests cost the same as simple ones — 1 credit whether you ask for a full site rebuild or just change the headline colour.

Credits are only deducted after a successful generation. If a generation fails, your credit is returned.
