We Built the Tool
We Wished Existed.

Beegen was born out of frustration — watching small businesses pay for five different tools that still didn't work together.

The Problem We Lived

Every small business we worked with was juggling the same nightmare: a website builder here, an email tool there, a separate funnel platform, a course system, and a CRM that didn't talk to any of them. They were spending $400–$600 a month on tools — and still couldn't get a clear picture of their business.

We watched talented entrepreneurs fail not from lack of hustle or a bad idea, but because their digital infrastructure was working against them. The platforms built for this existed — but they were built for enterprises, priced for enterprises, and required a developer to set up.

So we built Beegen. One platform. Every tool. One price. No engineers required.

Our Mission
"To give every small business owner the same powerful digital tools as the Fortune 500 — without the Fortune 500 price tag."

Simplicity First

Every feature we ship has to pass one test: can a non-technical business owner use it without reading a manual? If not, we go back to the drawing board.

Radical Transparency

Our pricing is on the website. Our roadmap is public. Our uptime stats are visible. We don't believe in hiding the ball from the people who trust us with their business.

Customer Obsession

When a customer wins, we win. Every product decision starts by asking: does this help our users grow their business? Not: does this help us grow our ARR.
