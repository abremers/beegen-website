Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Beegen ("we", "us", "our") operates beegen.co and app.beegen.co. We provide an AI-powered website builder. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hello@beegen.co.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account information (email address, password) when you sign up
  • Prompts and instructions you send to the AI builder
  • Brand assets you upload (logos, colour preferences)
  • Payment information processed securely via our payment provider

We also collect usage data automatically, including pages visited, features used, and device/browser information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Beegen service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, account notices)
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. AI Processing

Your prompts are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate website content. Anthropic's own privacy policy governs their handling of API data. We do not use your prompts or generated sites to train our own AI models.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing hello@beegen.co.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@beegen.co to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by email or via the app.
